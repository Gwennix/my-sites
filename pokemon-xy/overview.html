WELCOME TO THE KALOS REGION!

Smell the Galettes? see the fashion? In awe of a big shining tower? Then you know you're in the Kalos region!
Filled to the brim with flowers and fun shopping districts you can either skate or take a cab through the entire region to reach the nearest gym.
Visit all the different landmarks like the illumating Lumiose Tower or mysterious ruins at Geosenge Town making this an unforgettable journey!
